# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: July 2026**

This Privacy Policy explains how monkeygod.fun ("the Site," "we," "us," "our") collects, uses, and protects your information.

## 1. Information We Collect

- **Account information**: email address, username, and password (stored securely/hashed).
- **Payment information**: processed by third-party payment processors; we do not store full card numbers.
- **Usage data**: pages visited, device/browser type, IP address, and general analytics data.
- **Age verification data**: confirmation that you meet the minimum age requirement to access the Site.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:
- Provide and maintain the Site and its services.
- Process payments and manage subscriptions.
- Verify age and eligibility to access content.
- Respond to support requests and content removal requests.
- Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or illegal activity, including content involving minors.
- Comply with legal obligations.

## 3. Legal Basis / Compliance

We process personal data in a manner consistent with applicable data protection laws, including, where relevant, GDPR and CCPA principles. We retain records as required to comply with applicable laws governing adult content platforms.

## 4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

- Law enforcement, where required by law or in response to a valid legal request — including in connection with reports of content involving minors.
- Service providers who assist in operating the Site, under confidentiality obligations.

## 5. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law.
