# Refund Policy

**Effective Date: July 2026**

This Refund Policy applies to purchases, subscriptions, and paid content on monkeygod.fun ("the Site").

## 1. General Policy

Due to the digital and instantly-accessible nature of the content offered on this Site, **all sales are generally final** once content has been accessed, viewed, downloaded, or streamed.

## 2. Exceptions

Refunds may be considered on a case-by-case basis in the following situations:

- A duplicate or accidental charge.
- A technical failure that prevented you from accessing purchased content, where the issue could not be resolved through support.
- Unauthorized charges resulting from account compromise (subject to verification).

## 3. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, contact us via Telegram: [https://telegram.me/cynski](https://telegram.me/cynski) within 7 days of the transaction. Include your transaction ID/reference and a description of the issue.

## 4. Subscription Cancellations

You may cancel a recurring subscription at any time. Cancellation stops future billing but does not automatically entitle you to a refund for the current billing period unless it falls under Section 2.

## 5. Chargebacks

Filing a chargeback without first contacting us to resolve the issue may result in suspension or termination of your account. We encourage you to reach out via Telegram first so we can resolve the matter directly.

## 6. Processing Time

Approved refunds will be processed to the original payment method within a reasonable timeframe, subject to the policies of the relevant payment processor.

## 7. Contact

For all refund inquiries, contact us via Telegram: [https://telegram.me/cynski](https://telegram.me/cynski)
